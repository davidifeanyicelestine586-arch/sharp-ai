Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 5, 2026

At Sharp AI, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our SaaS platform.

1. Data Collection

We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our services:

  • API Credentials: Your OpenAI API keys are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage and are never sent to our servers except to proxy requests to the AI provider.
  • Content Inputs: The topics and prompts you enter are used solely to generate your content and are not used to train our internal models without explicit consent.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data (e.g., number of generations) to improve our platform performance.

2. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Client-side encryption for sensitive tokens.
  • Regular security audits of our infrastructure.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party AI providers (such as OpenAI) to process your content. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details on how they handle data processed through their APIs.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have stored about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@sharp-ai.com.
