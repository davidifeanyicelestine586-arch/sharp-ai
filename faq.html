Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about Sharp AI.

How does Sharp AI differ from ChatGPT?

While ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI, Sharp AI is a specialized content studio. We use custom frameworks and platform-native blueprints (like LinkedIn post structures and X threads) specifically engineered for high performance and conversion.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We have transitioned to a high-security server-side model. Your AI interactions are encrypted and processed through our secure backend, meaning you no longer need to manage or store sensitive API keys in your browser's local storage.

What happens if I run out of free generations?

Free accounts are limited to 3 generations to test the platform. Once exhausted, you can upgrade to our Pro plan for unlimited generations and access to premium templates.

Can I export my content?

Absolutely. You can copy individual blocks (Hook, Body, CTA) or use the "Export Bundle" feature to download the entire generation as a formatted text file.

Does Sharp AI support languages other than English?

Currently, Sharp AI is optimized for English, but the underlying models support over 50 languages. You can input your topic in your preferred language, and the AI will respond accordingly.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

You can manage your subscription directly from the Settings panel under your account profile.
