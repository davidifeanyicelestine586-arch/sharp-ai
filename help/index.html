Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from the Billing section in your Settings panel. Your pro features will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.

Can I use my own OpenAI API key?

Yes! Go to Settings and enter your key. This will bypass our usage limits and use your own credits directly.

Is there a mobile app?

Sharp AI is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can "Add to Home Screen" on your iOS or Android device for a native-like experience.
